How It Works

While browsing Facebook Marketplace listings, a blue "Save Listing" button appears in the bottom-right corner. Click it to open this extension and review the auto-extracted data before saving.

On Facebook group pages, a "Save to Apartment Search" button appears below rental posts.

All listings are sent to your local backend and appear in the Search tab of the app.
